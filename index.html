Do They Know It's Christmas?

No? Well, let them know… with a printed christmas card from your browser

1. Enter your personal christmas message • 2. Click on "Print" • 3. Disable all headers, footers and margins • 4. Enable background images • 5. Print in landscape orientation • 6. Have a merry christmas
